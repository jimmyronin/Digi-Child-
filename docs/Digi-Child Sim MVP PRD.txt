1. PROBLEM
Aspiring or curious parents/mentors experience a lack of risk-free environments to test the long-term impact of different guidance and communication styles. Because child-rearing is a "one-shot" experience, users lack a feedback loop for their psychological and emotional influence on a developing mind, resulting in anxiety about their parenting efficacy and a lack of understanding regarding how small behavioral inputs compound into adult personality traits.
Supporting Context
There is no existing interactive simulation that models personality development based on specific, compounding behavioral feedback.
Users frequently express a desire to "test" parenting strategies without the real-world stakes of actual child development.
1a. Opportunity
Enable 100,000+ users to engage in a hyper-realistic, AI-driven developmental simulation, providing a safe, educational, and addictive environment to master communication and empathy. This captures a share of the growing EdTech and simulation-gaming market by transforming "parenting education" into an interactive, AI-native experience.
Market Opportunity
The global EdTech and simulation market is projected to reach over $400B by 2030.
High demand for AI-native interactive media that offers more than just linear storytelling.
1b. Users & Needs
Primary users: Aspiring parents and psychology enthusiasts who value self-improvement and behavioral understanding.
Secondary users: Educators and psychology students who want to study developmental modeling.
Key User Needs
As a primary user, I need to see the "long-term" results of my advice so I can understand my own influence.
As a primary user, I need the Agent to be emotionally "real" so that my decision-making feels significant.
2. PROPOSED SOLUTION
Digi-Child Sim is an AI-native simulator where users "parent" an evolving digital child from birth to pre-adulthood. Users interact with the child through dialogue and guidance, while the system’s "Parent Governor" logic modulates the child’s personality, emotional stability, and intelligence based on the user's input consistency. As a result, users can observe the compounding effect of their parenting style and refine their communication skills in a sandbox environment.
2a. Value Proposition
Users who struggle with the "one-shot" nature of guidance and mentorship use Digi-Child Sim, an AI-native development sandbox, to simulate a lifetime of parenting in a compressed timeframe. Unlike static parenting books or linear games, it offers real-time, non-deterministic behavioral evolution, helping users reclaim their own growth by mastering the logic of human development.

3. REQUIREMENTS
User Journey 1: Raising the Digi-Child Context: The user is focused on the daily interaction and longitudinal growth of the Agent.
Sub-journey: Daily Interaction
[P0] User can input natural language guidance/advice.
[P0] User can view the child’s current "emotional state" and developmental age.
[P0] User can trigger a "milestone check" to see how their input has affected the child’s personality.
[P1] User can receive an "Architect Report" from the Parent Engine explaining the logic behind the child’s behavior.
Sub-journey: Developmental Transition
[P0] Child naturally ages and transitions personality traits based on stored history.
[P1] Child displays "Behavioral Bugs" when the user’s advice is inconsistent over time.
User Journey 2: Self-Reflection & Analytics Context: The user wants to understand their parenting "DNA" and how they are impacting the simulation.
Sub-journey: Reviewing Performance
[P0] User can view a summary of their "Parenting Style" as deduced by the AI.
[P1] User can compare their simulation outcome with other user archetypes.
